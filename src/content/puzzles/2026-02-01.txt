Title
---
Oopsie at the Oscars

Puzzle
---
Amy Poehler: Tina! You dropped all the cue cards! We’re supposed to go on and announce the winner in thirty seconds!

Tina Fey: Okay, okay, let’s think. Two of us were just gonna do jokes, and one of us reads out the winner.

Maya Rudolph: I know I’m the one mentioning Michael B Jordan. Am I joking about his dating life?

Tina Fey: No that joke was not about him.

Amy Poehler: I remember there’s a joke about nobody seeing Marty Supreme. 

Announcer: Up next to present the award for Best Actor, Tina Fey, Amy Poehler and Maya Rudolph!

Maya Rudolph: Ugh, Tina, why did you have to drop the cards? We need to go on now!

Tina Fey: I was too busy visualizing looking straight into Leo DiCaprio’s eyes as I say his name.

Question
---
Who is winning Best Actor?

Options
---
Michael B Jordan
Timothee Chalamet
Leonardo DiCaprio

Answer
---
1

Explanation
---
Tina Fey makes the dating joke about Leonardo DiCaprio.
Amy Poehler makes fun of Timothee Chalamet's movie.
Maya Rudolph reads out the winner, Michael B. Jordan.
